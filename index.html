RADIATOR LEAK

Your vehicle’s radiator may leak suddenly and leave you stranded on the side of the road. The most common reasons for a leak can be a hole in the radiator caused by rust, a manufacturing defect, or cracks developing from the engine getting too hot. Regardless of what caused your radiator to leak, it’s never wise to drive your car after it overheats.
